Annexure - E.4

Financial Solvency

(applicable for grant of AEO-T1, AEO-T2, AEO-T3 and AEO-LO)

E.4 Compliance Yes No Remarks
a) Whether the applicant has been financially solvent during the three financial years preceding the date of application? (Solvency would generally be defined as good financial standing that is sufficient to fulfill the commitments of the applicant including ability to pay duties)
Please attach Balance Sheets for last three Financial Years.
b) Whether, where required, the accounts have been filed with Registrar of Companies within the time limits laid down by law?
c) Whether, where applicable, audit qualifications or comments in the annual accounts about the continuation of the business as a going concern?
d) Whether there are any contingent liabilities or provisions?
e) Whether the net current assets are positive?
f) Whether the entity has been defaulted in payment of due taxes during the past three years?
g) Whether there is solvency certificate issued by the Statutory Auditor? If yes, provide the copy.
h) Whether the business entity is currently listed as insolvent, or in liquidation or bankruptcy?
